About Vertex Metals

A trade-focused intermediary, built for the India–UK metals corridor

Vertex Metals Ltd is incorporated on the Isle of Man and structured to trade industrial metals and strategic minerals between Indian manufacturers and UK industrial buyers — with compliance, documentation, and substance at the core of everything we do.

Company

What we do

Vertex Metals Ltd is a commodity trading intermediary. We do not hold physical stock — we act as principal in trades, sourcing metals from Indian manufacturers and supplying them to UK industrial buyers under documented contracts.

Our initial focus is Aluminium Alloy Core Wire (EC Grade and 6XXX series), a critical input for EV cabling, overhead transmission lines, and aerospace wiring systems. India's market share in UK aluminium wire imports grew by 2,615% year-on-year — we are structured to meet that demand while providing the compliance standards UK procurement teams require.

Phase 2 of our product roadmap includes copper concentrate and lithium-ion battery precursor materials.

Trading Model

How our intermediary model works

We act as principal in every trade. UK buyers have a single, compliant counterparty to deal with. Indian suppliers have a reliable, qualified buyer.

01

Source & Qualify

We identify and qualify suppliers in India against our AML/KYC and quality standards. All counterparties are subject to sanctions screening before any trade.

02

Price RFQs

UK buyers submit requests for quotation. We obtain competitive FOB quotes from qualified suppliers and provide CIF UK port pricing under CETA tariff rates.

03

Execute Trade

We act as principal — purchasing from the Indian supplier and selling to the UK buyer. We manage Letters of Credit, Bills of Lading, certificates of origin, and all trade documentation.

04

Logistics & Delivery

We coordinate sea freight, customs clearance, and delivery to the UK buyer's specified destination, maintaining oversight at every stage of transit.

Quality & Standards

Built to ISO 9001 principles from day one

Vertex Metals' systems, processes, and trade documentation are designed to comply with ISO 9001 quality management principles from the outset of operations. We are not retrofitting compliance after the fact — we are building the way we want to be audited from the start.

ISO 9001 Accreditation — targeted after year one of operations

Our trade documentation workflows, supplier qualification processes, order management procedures, and internal review cycles are all structured to ISO 9001 requirements. We are actively pursuing formal accreditation following our first full year of trading. The accreditation will validate a way of working that is already embedded in how we operate.

GDPR Compliant

All buyer and supplier personal data is handled in full compliance with UK GDPR. Data is not shared with third parties without consent and is not retained beyond its legitimate business purpose.

Full Regulatory Framework

AML/KYC, sanctions screening, CBAM, EORI, VAT, and Isle of Man Companies Act compliance are all embedded into our operating structure — not managed reactively when an issue arises.

Trade Documentation Standard

Every trade is documented to the standard expected by UK procurement, legal, and finance teams: bills of lading, certificates of origin, mill test reports, and CBAM carbon-intensity data where applicable.
