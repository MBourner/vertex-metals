Sustainability

A lighter footprint, built into the model

Our no-warehouse model isn't just operationally efficient — it genuinely reduces carbon output compared to traditional stock-holding metals traders. No warehouse energy. No overstock. No unnecessary handling.

  • No warehouse = no associated energy consumption or embodied carbon in surplus stock
  • CBAM-ready supplier data collection — reduces your reporting burden from 2027
  • Responsible sourcing standards for mica, lithium, and high-risk minerals
  • Carbon-intensity data from Indian supplier facilities, available on request
Our sustainability approach →

CBAM — an opportunity, not a burden

UK CBAM takes effect January 2027. Buyers who source through Vertex Metals gain access to pre-collected carbon-intensity data — simplifying your own compliance obligations before reporting kicks in.

ISO 9001 — targeted post year one

Our systems and processes are designed to ISO 9001 principles from day one. We are actively pursuing accreditation after our first full year of operations.

Responsible sourcing by policy

Mica is sourced exclusively from suppliers with certified responsible-sourcing credentials. ESG due diligence is a mandatory part of supplier onboarding across all product categories.
