Industry Sectors

The industries our metals power

UK industrial buyers across energy, automotive, aerospace, construction, telecoms, electronics, and renewables rely on aluminium alloys, copper products, stainless steel, and critical minerals. Vertex Metals sources across all of these sectors from qualified global suppliers.

Energy & Power Infrastructure

Powering the UK grid and renewable rollout

The UK's energy transition is accelerating demand for aluminium alloy core wire and copper products on an unprecedented scale. Overhead transmission line upgrades, new grid connections for offshore wind, and expansion of distribution networks are all driving procurement of large volumes of aluminium conductor wire and copper cable.

Vertex Metals' launch product — Aluminium Alloy Core Wire (EC Grade and 6XXX series) — is a critical input for this sector. Sourced from Gujarat and Odisha under the India–UK CETA, we can supply at competitive CIF UK port pricing with full documentation and CBAM data.

  • Overhead transmission line conductors (aluminium alloy core wire)
  • Distribution network infrastructure
  • Offshore and onshore wind interconnects (copper cable)
  • Substation and switchgear components (copper)
  • Solar farm cabling (copper & aluminium)

Automotive & Electric Vehicles

The West Midlands EV supply chain

The UK automotive sector — centred on the West Midlands but extending to Sunderland, Derby, and beyond — is undergoing a major transition to electric vehicle production. EV wiring harnesses are significantly more complex than those in ICE vehicles, driving sharply higher demand for aluminium alloy wire and copper cable inputs.

Vertex Metals is directly positioned to serve this demand. Our aluminium alloy core wire sourced from Gujarat and Odisha is a direct input for EV wiring harness manufacturers across the UK Midlands corridor.

  • EV wiring harnesses (aluminium alloy wire, copper)
  • Power cable manufacture for EV charging infrastructure
  • Aluminium structural components (body-in-white, battery enclosures)
  • Copper busbars and connectors
  • Lithium battery precursor materials (forward capability)

Aerospace & Defence

Precision materials for aerospace manufacture

UK aerospace manufacturing — centred on Bristol, Derby, Belfast, and Broughton — demands materials with certified quality, documented traceability, and consistent specification. Aluminium alloy profiles and structural forms, high-grade stainless steel, and specialist nickel superalloys are all requirements of this sector.

Jackson Paul's background in aerospace component machining gives Vertex Metals a genuine understanding of the quality standards, tolerances, and documentation requirements that aerospace procurement teams demand. We source to specification and supply with full mill certification.

  • Aluminium alloy profiles (structural, wiring)
  • Stainless steel bar and sheet (airframe components)
  • Nickel superalloys including Inconel grades
  • Titanium (non-India origin, with origin verification)
  • Precision aluminium and copper products to spec

Telecommunications & Technology

Materials for network infrastructure and electronics

Copper cable has been the backbone of telecommunications infrastructure for over a century and remains critical for distribution networks, data centres, and 5G rollout. Aluminium aerial wire is used extensively in overhead telecoms infrastructure. Mica and lithium-ion battery precursor materials serve the electronics and technology supply chain.

  • Copper cable and rod (distribution and network infrastructure)
  • Aluminium aerial wire (overhead telecoms)
  • Mica (electrical insulation in electronics)
  • Copper tube and sheet (heat exchangers, data centre cooling)
  • Lithium battery precursors (battery backup systems)

Industrial Manufacturing

Process industries and general manufacturing

UK process industries — chemicals, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, oil and gas — require stainless steel grades with specific corrosion-resistance properties and certified documentation. Vertex Metals sources to specification from qualified mills. We can obtain material data sheets, mill test certificates, and quality certifications as required by your procurement process.

  • Copper tube and fittings for process engineering

  • Manganese, chromite, and other minerals for specialist industrial processes
