Aluminium Alloys

Gujarat & Odisha, India · Secondary: Europe, China

Our extensive network of reputable mills across India specialises in aluminium alloys of the highest quality across all grades. Our launch focus is Aluminium Alloy Core Wire (EC Grade and 6XXX series) — a critical input for EV cabling, overhead transmission infrastructure, and aerospace wiring systems. All products are supplied under the India–UK CETA tariff advantage and are accompanied by full documentation including certificates of origin and mill test reports.

UK Market Context

India's market share in UK aluminium alloy wire grew 2,615% year-on-year. Aluminium was designated a UK Critical Mineral in March 2025. Near tariff-free access under India–UK CETA signed May 2025.

Our Process

How we qualify suppliers and source to spec

Every product we supply is sourced from a qualified supplier. Every supplier is subject to the same rigorous onboarding process before any trade is executed.

01

Receive RFQ

You submit your specification, quantity, delivery timeline, and required certifications. We confirm whether we can source within your parameters.

02

Supplier Qualification

We apply AML/KYC, sanctions screening, and quality due diligence to every supplier. No new supplier is used without completing this process.

03

Quote & Contract

We provide a CIF UK port price structured to leverage applicable trade-agreement tariff rates. Contracts are executed under established trade terms (LC or bank transfer).

04

Delivery & Docs

We manage sea freight, customs clearance, and documentation — Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, mill test reports, and CBAM data where applicable.
