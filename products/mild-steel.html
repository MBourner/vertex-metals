Mild Steel

On Enquiry

High-quality mild steel in all standard forms — from structural sections to flat products and tubular forms — sourced from qualified mills across India and the Asia-Pacific.

Overview

Vertex Metals sources high-quality mild steel from established manufacturers across India and the Asia-Pacific. With strong and established mill connections, we ensure a seamless supply chain for this versatile metal — delivered directly to UK buyers with full documentation and compliance built in.

Mild steel is the backbone of UK structural and civil engineering, construction, fabrication, automotive, and industrial manufacturing. We source to specification across all standard structural grades — from S235 through to S355 and ASTM equivalents.

All products are supplied with mill test reports and certificates of origin as standard.

UK Market Context

UK mild steel demand is driven by infrastructure investment, construction activity, and industrial manufacturing. Procurement teams require consistent specification, reliable lead times, and competitive landed cost. Vertex Metals provides a qualified, compliant sourcing route with full documentation from mill to UK port.
