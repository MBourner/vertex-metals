Other Metals & Alloys

On Enquiry

Brass, bronze, tool steel, Inconel, and titanium sourced from our global qualified supplier network against your specific requirements — with full AML/KYC and documentation standards applied to every transaction.

Overview

Vertex Metals' global sourcing capability extends beyond our core metal families to a range of specialist metals and alloys. Whether you require brass for electrical connectors, bronze for marine bearings, tool steel for die manufacturing, or Inconel for high-temperature applications, we source against your specification from our qualified global supplier network.

Every enquiry is assessed against our full AML/KYC framework prior to commitment. We do not source from unqualified or unscreened suppliers, regardless of material category.

Titanium is sourced exclusively from non-India origins, with full origin verification documentation provided.

How We Source

For materials outside our active inventory categories, we work to your specification and timeline. Submit your requirements — including grade, form, quantity, and required certifications — and our sourcing team will confirm feasibility, supplier qualification status, and indicative pricing within one to two business days.

Typical Applications

Brass — electrical connectors, valves, fittings
Bronze — bearings, bushings, marine applications
Tool steel — dies, moulds, cutting tools
Inconel — high-temperature industrial plant
Titanium — specialist engineering (non-India origin)
Bright steel — precision engineering & shafting

Sourcing commitment

We do not commit to sourcing until supplier qualification, AML/KYC, and sanctions screening are complete. This protects you as the buyer — every supplier we use has been vetted before goods are ordered.
