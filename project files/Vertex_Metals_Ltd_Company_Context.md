## Background & Business Purpose

Vertex Metals Ltd is being established as a commodity trading intermediary, incorporated on the Isle of Man, connecting metal and mineral exporters in India with buyers in the United Kingdom. The company will act as principal in trades — sourcing, facilitating logistics, handling documentation, and earning a margin — rather than physically holding stock.

### Key founding assets

- **Jackson Paul** brings extensive manufacturing and mining contacts in India, a track record running a steel import business from the Isle of Man (connecting Indian manufacturers with UK buyers), and prior engagement with the UK Chamber of Commerce on lithium import supply chains.
- **Martyn Bourner** brings expertise in air transport of dangerous goods (directly applicable to lithium and other hazardous mineral shipments), software development, web development, and project management. Martyn will lead technical infrastructure development including supplier/buyer systems and compliance documentation tooling.

---

## Initial Trading Focus

The MVP product focus is **Aluminium Alloy Core Wire** (EC Grade / 6XXX series), selected on the basis of:

- 2,615% year-on-year growth in India's market share for this product in the UK
- Aluminium's designation as a UK Critical Mineral (added March 2025)
- Strong demand from West Midlands automotive (EV cabling) and aerospace sectors
- Moderate barriers to entry compared to precious metals or regulated chemicals

Planned supplier sourcing from manufacturing clusters in **Gujarat** (Ahmedabad) and **Odisha**, India.

### Expansion roadmap (post-MVP)

- **Phase 2:** Strategic minerals — copper concentrate, lithium-ion battery precursors
- **Phase 3:** Precious metals and gems — lab-grown diamonds, silver jewellery
